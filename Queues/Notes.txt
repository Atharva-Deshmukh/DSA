USES OF QUEUES:

Regular queue
-------------
- CPU resource scheduling

Deques (Double-Ended Queues)
----------------------------
- Recently visited browser pages (Forward and Backward Navigation)

Circular Queues
---------------

OS Process Scheduling -> Round Robin Scheduling and Traffic Light System

Priority Queues
----------------

OS Process Scheduling (Priority Scheduling) -> Example: Real-time systems (air traffic control, medical systems).
